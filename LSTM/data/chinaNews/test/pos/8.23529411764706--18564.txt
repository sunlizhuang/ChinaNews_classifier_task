China lauds Pakistan's efforts for regional peace

Chinese Interior Minister will visit Pakistan soon.

This was disclosed by Chinese ambassador to Islamabad Yao Jing today during a meeting with interior minister Ijaz Shah in Islamabad today (Wednesday).

Issues pertaining to bilateral relations and security issues of China and Pakistan came under discussion.

Interior Minister said this visit is of vital importance for strengthening bilateral relations between Pakistan and China.

Chinese Ambassador said Pakistan's efforts for peace in the region are commendable.
