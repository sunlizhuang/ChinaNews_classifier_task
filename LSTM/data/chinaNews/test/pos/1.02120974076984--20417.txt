Compression therapy is used for patients with venous ulcers. This therapy is used to increase blood flow activity in the lower limbs through strengthening vein support. It helps to improve aching and release compression between the vein walls. It is a simple and effective mechanical principle that includes applying an elastic garment around the leg. This therapy is used to treat phlebitis, thrombosis, post vein surgery as well as to release all manifestations of chronic venous disease such as heavy legs, varicose veins, oedema, and leg ulcers. The increasing rate of chronic venous is driving the global compression therapy market growth.
