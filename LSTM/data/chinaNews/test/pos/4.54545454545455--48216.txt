Minister Lawrence Wong on Monday (Feb 3) thanked the many Singaporeans who have come forward to support those affected by the novel coronavirus. These include the Scoot pilots and crew who volunteered to fly 92 Singaporeans back from Wuhan, as well as students from the National University of Singapore (NUS) and Nanyang Technological University (NTU) who delivered food to their friends on Leave of Absence.
