Foreign Minister Shah Mehmood Qureshi on Friday in a telephonic conversation with his Chinese counterpart Wang Xi briefly discussed the impacts of coronovirus outbreak along with the areas affected by it.

Chinese FM Xi apprised Qureshi about the affected areas and the government’s security measures to prevent the deadly virus. He said that the Wealth Health Organization (WHO) has praised the Chinese government’s effort in the battle against coronavirus. He added that their government is facilitating the Pakistani community with everything as their own citizens.

He assured the FM that they are using all the resources to protect the Pakistani students in China.

FM Qureshi expressed his gratitude towards the Chinese authorities for dealing with the epidemic and helping Pakistanis, especially students. He said that the people and government of Pakistan stand with China in this difficult time.
