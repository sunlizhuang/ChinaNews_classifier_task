On Feb. 26 at 5:30 p.m. the Twain House & Museum’s free West End Wednesdays series, which features authors who live in or near the West End of Hartford, will present a talk by Ethan Rutherford and poets Clare Rossini and Ciaran Berry. Rutherford is the author of “The Peripatetic Coffin and Other Stories.” His short fiction has appeared in Tin House, Ploughshares, American Short Fiction and The Best American Short Stories. All three teach at Trinity College. The event begin at 5:30 p.m. with refreshments and cash-only wine and beer. Readings and discussions will start at 6 p.m.
