Aeroflot prolongs free return of tickets to China till March

MOSCOW, Jan 30 (PRIME) -- Russia’s biggest airline Aeroflot has prolonged free return and replacement of tickets to China until the end of February, the company said in a statement on Thursday.

“Aeroflot prolongs the period of return and replacement of tickets to all destinations from and to China: Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Hong Kong, Harbin, and in the framework of code sharing with the Chinese partners of Aeroflot China Eastern and China Southern (Sanya, Dalian, Wuhan), and partners in interline agreements,” the airline said.

The passengers with flights scheduled until February 29 can change the tickets to any date until March 31.
