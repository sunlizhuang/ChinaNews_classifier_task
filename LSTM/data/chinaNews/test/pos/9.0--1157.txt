Shanghai has been crowned the most innovative city in the Yangtze River Delta region, according to rankings released by the China Academy of Urban Planning and Design Shanghai Branch.

From the perspective of innovation, the ranking assessed 41 cities in the Yangtze River Delta region using monitoring data based on two dimensions and 10 indexes to better reflect the personality and potential of every city.

In terms of innovation strength, Jiangsu province reported strong performance this year, accounting for half of the top 10 cities.
