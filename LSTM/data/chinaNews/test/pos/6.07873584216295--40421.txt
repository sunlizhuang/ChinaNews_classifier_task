The Office of China Programs and Department of Modern and Classical Languages will host the 2020 Chinese New Year Celebration Week from Jan. 29-31. There will be a series of Chinese cultural activities from Chinese martial arts and dance performances, to dumpling cooking workshops. Attendees will get hands-on experience in most of the activities.

Ningxia University faculty will make Chinese dumplings with attendees. Attendees will also enjoy the dumplings they made at the end of the workshop.

It will be a great activity to wrap up the 2020 Chinese New Year Celebration Week!
