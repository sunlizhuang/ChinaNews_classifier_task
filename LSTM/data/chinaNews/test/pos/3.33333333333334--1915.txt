The Moscow branch houses two fascinating projects under one roof. Vladivostok 3000 is a Russian restaurant and the house specialty is Pacific cuisine, including Pacific whelk, fern bracken, sea cucumber, laminaria, fern, and other delicacies, as well as a unique offer of Asian drinks: Korean soju, Chinese beer, Japanese whiskey, etc. The Mumiy Troll Music Bar is a music bar with excellent cocktails and dance floor, where Russian and foreign bands and DJ's perform every night. Located in the heart of the capital, this is a great place for meeting friends and having a good time, 24/7.
