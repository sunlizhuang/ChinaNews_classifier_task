BEIJING, Jan. 13 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Vice Premier Han Zheng will attend the 2020 World Economic Forum (WEF) Annal Meeting in Davos, Switzerland, and pay a visit to Switzerland from Jan. 20 to 21, a Foreign Ministry spokesperson announced Monday.

Han was invited by Klaus Schwab, the founder and executive chairman of the WEF, and Vice President of the Swiss Confederation Guy Parmelin, said Geng Shuang, the spokesperson. ■
