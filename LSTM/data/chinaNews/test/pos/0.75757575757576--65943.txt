A British comic actor known for his resemblance to Mr Bean has extended his visit to Wuhan, the centre of the coronavirus outbreak, in a show of solidarity with the city’s residents.

Nigel Dixon, 53, from Felixstowe, Suffolk, travelled to Wuhan at the start of last month with plans to celebrate the lunar new year. As the virus spread, rather than returning home as advised by his family, he chose to stay in the quarantined city of 11 million people.

“I thought if I leave Wuhan, it’s possible that I can spread the virus to others. I thought it would be selfish of me,” he said in a video shown by the state broadcaster.
