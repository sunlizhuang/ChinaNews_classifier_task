Year 11 and 12 international Chinese students to be allowed to return to Australia with quarantine restrictions

Duration: 03:00

February 22, 2020. Federal Education Minister Dan Tehan, and Victorian Education Minister James Merlino announce that year 11 and 12 secondary students from China will be allowed to return to Australia under strict quarantine guidelines. Australia's Chief Medical Office Brendan Murphy says that there is no threat to the community if procesdures are followed. (AAP Video/Alex Murray)
