by Todd Epp

WASHINGTON (KELO.com) -- During his lengthy introduction before signing the U.S.-China Phase 1 Trade Agreement this morning, President Donald Trump gave a "shoutout" to Sen. Mike Rounds.

Trump said that Rounds was constantly calling him about the status of negotiations and the importance of getting the agreement done for South Dakota agricultural producers.

The President also gave a "shout out" to Sen. John Thune.

There are at least four other South Dakotans in attendance at the White House ceremony.

They include Gov. Kristi Noem with two South Dakota farmers and South Dakota Farm Bureau President Scott VanderWal.
