Ugbest Electric Scooter is one of the leading brands of the Chinese electric scooter industry. Taizhou Ugbest EV Co., Ltd, went into electric scooter business in 2012.

We have successfully developed a series of classic models, including Romex, Vax, Pony, and Legend, cooperating with Italian brand EcobIt. Now Ugbest Electric Scooter has more than 30 models, among which 10 models are our patent models. We have 7 models with a new Euro 4 EEC certificate. To ensure quality, Ugbest has sufficient QC members to carry out an inspection in every stage. The factory is in accordance with ISO90001 quality management system and ISO 14001 environmental management system as well as OHSAS 18000.
