Elegant swans travel for thousands of miles to Rongcheng in E China's Shandong to spend a joyful winter.

RONGCHENG, Jan. 13, 2020 (Xinhua) -- Rongcheng Swan Lake, known as the "hometown of swan", is an important relay station for the southward migration of the birds due to its mild temperature.
