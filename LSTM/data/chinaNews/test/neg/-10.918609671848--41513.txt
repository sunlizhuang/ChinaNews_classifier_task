Two doctors at a Wuhan hospital were reportedly beaten by the family of a coronavirus patient who died.

State-run China News published a photo purportedly showing the injuries on a doctor’s neck as a result of the alleged assault at Wuhan’s No. 4 People’s Hospital on Wednesday.

Police said their face masks and hazardous materials suits were also torn apart in the attack, Beijing-based Caixin reported.

One of the doctors sustained fractures and had to have a cast, Beijing Youth Daily reported.

The suspect, simply identified as Ke, was taken into police custody.

The coronavirus epidemic has infected nearly 10,000 people worldwide, prompting the World Health Organization to declare the outbreak a global emergency.

The vast majority of the cases have been in China’s Hubei province and its provincial capital, Wuhan, where the first illnesses were detected in December.

The virus has claimed the lives of 213 people in China, but no deaths have been reported outside the country.
