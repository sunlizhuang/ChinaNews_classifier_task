Wenlock Hospital, Mangaluru

MANGALURU: Even as death toll rises due to Coronavirus infection in China, District Wenlock Hospital in the city too has made arrangements to treat patients since the city has an international airport.Wenlock Hospital medical superintendent Dr HR Rajeshwari Devi told TOI that an isolation ward has been kept ready at the hospital.“We have guidelines related to all respiratory virus infections and accordingly, we have kept an isolation ward ready. Other preventive measures like masks for staff have also been arranged. We have made preparations so that any suspected cases can be admitted and treatment can be started in the isolation ward as per national guidelines,” Dr Rajeshwari said.Sources in health department said, no direction has come to open screening counter at Mangaluru International Airport since there are no direct flights to Mangaluru from China.
