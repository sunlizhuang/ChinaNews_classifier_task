Tokayev says Kazakhstan is ready to provide humanitarian aid to China amid virus outbreak

AKIPRESS.COM - Kazakhstani President Kassym-Jomart Tokayev sent a letter to President Xi Jinping on the spread of coronavirus, Akorda reported.



Highly appreciating the decisions and actions of the Chinese leadership to localize and curb the dangerous virus, Tokayev informed the Chinese President on the decision of the Kazakh government to provide all possible humanitarian assistance to China if necessary.

The head of state also reported on measures taken by the Government to prevent the spread of coronavirus in Kazakhstan.
