South Korea has reported ITS first suspected case of a mysterious pneumonia-like illness that has been spreading in China.According to the Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention on Wednesday, a 36-year-old Chinese woman who had visited the Chinese city of Wuhan last month, showed symptoms of the illness.The patient has been isolated and is currently undergoing treatment after checking into a local hospital in Dongtan, some 50 kilometers south of Seoul.Beijing says the pneumonia-like illness does not seem to be transmittable between people.
