The Coronavirus epidemic that has gripped China has left the death toll in the country touching 80 with its National Health Commission reporting close to 2,800* confirmed cases to date.

Fear of people contacting the virus has risen sharply and countries are working toward lifting their citizens from China at the earliest.

The commission also termed the condition of the 461 people being treated for the virus, officially known as 2019-nCoV, as "critical." It said that during the past 24 hours, 769 new confirmed cases, 3,806 new suspected cases and 24 deaths from the disease were reported.

By Sunday, the pneumonia-like illness was responsible for a total of 80 deaths, while 51 people had reportedly recovered, and 5,794 remained as suspected patients, state-run Xinhua news agency quoted the commission as saying.

A total of 32,799 close contacts have been traced, the commission said, adding 30,453 among them were currently under medical observation while 583 others were discharged on Sunday.

A suspected case of coronavirus has also been reported in Bihar.

According to the Superintendent, Patna Medical College and Hospital (PMCH), a girl hailing from Chapra, who recently returned from China, was admitted to ICU at a hospital in Chapra after she showed symptoms similar to that of Coronavirus.

Another suspected case of coronavirus has been reported from Rajasthan.
