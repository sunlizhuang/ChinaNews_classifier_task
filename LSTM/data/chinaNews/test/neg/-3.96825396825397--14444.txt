‘Rawat’s statement exposes Indian designs against Kashmiris’

The Chairman of International Commission for Human Rights, Abdul Majeed Tramboo and Chairman of South Asia Centre for Peace and Human Rights, Professor Nazir Ahmad Shawl, have strongly condemned the provocative and dangerous statement of the newly appointed India’s Chief of Defence Staff Bipin Rawat.

Barrister Tramboo and Professor Shawl in their statements in London said Indian designs are to torture physically and mentally Kashmiri boys who are being detained and remain still unknown places mostly after 5th August 2019.

The statements asked the international community including the United Nations, the US, the UK, the EU and China to take notice of the worst situation and might policy of India in this world’s most militarized zone, Kashmir.
