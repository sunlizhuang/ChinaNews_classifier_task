Sweden reports 1st imported novel coronavirus case

By NewsDesk @infectiousdiseasenews

The Swedish Public Health Authority reported Friday (computer translated) the first imported novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV) case in Jönköping County.

The case is a woman who visited the Wuhan area in China, and who had symptoms in the form of cough, after arriving in Sweden. She landed in Sweden on January 24 and was then symptom free. The woman contacted the care herself.

The Jönköping County Region has followed the guidelines that exist regarding suspected infection and the woman is currently isolated at the infection clinic. The woman had symptoms in the form of cough and she is not seriously ill.

The Public Health Authority has carried out about 20 analyzes of samples from people who have had respiratory symptoms after staying in the affected area in China.
