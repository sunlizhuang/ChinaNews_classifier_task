With the fast-spreading Coronavirus taking the lives of many across the world, the world Health Organisation has announced health emergency. The novel Coronavirus have reportedly entered India as well with two cases In Kerala.

On the other hand, there is a severe concern as the 58 Telugu engineers have stuck in the Wuhan as part of their job training. However, the government of India has taken initiative and sent an Indian airline flight to bring back the employees. The employees have undergone Coronavirus tests at the Nepal border.

In this backdrop, an employee named Shruti was detained in Wuhan for suffering from severe fever at the time of her departure. Officers also blocked another employee along with her. The victim expressed her concern and sent a video message to her mother. She said she was not diagnosed with Coronavirus.
