1. Chinese doctor who first found out about coronavirus was censored. Now, he's dead.

A Chinese doctor who first warned his government of the coronavirus (2019-nCoV) outbreak but had his claims dismissed, was himself infected and is now dead. Li Wenliang, 34, was an ophthalmologist at the Wuhan Central Hospital; he told his friends about a viral infection spreading through the district via private messages as early as December 30. The Chinese government said he was spreading rumours.
