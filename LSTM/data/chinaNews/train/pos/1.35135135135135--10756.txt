Janine Chang:Everyone is obsessed with Roy Chiu

(Beijing January, 13) Sicheng Chen, Roy Chiu and Janine Chang attended the press conference to promote their new TV series “Detective Chinatown” in Beijing today. Talked about Roy‘s flirting skills in the series, Yusi Chen said: “When we were shooting in Thailand, the young people, no matter boys or girls, were crazy for him”. Janine said: “His eyes are so charming. Even males are obsessed with him”.
