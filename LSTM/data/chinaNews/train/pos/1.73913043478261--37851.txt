Rapper and actor Chris “Ludacris” Bridges, teamed up with Stubhub for a very special surprise.



Students at a high school in Miami received a donation of more than $75,000 in musical instruments. The band director received a pair of tickets to the Super Bowl.

The students got a Q&A with Ludacris. It was all part of Stubhub’s ticket forward program.

They have donated $3.5 million of musical instruments to high schools throughout the U.S.
