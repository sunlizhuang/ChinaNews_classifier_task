China will continue to work with WHO to safeguard health security

China says it will continue to work with World Health Organization and other countries to safeguard regional and global public health security.

Chinese Foreign Ministry’s Spokesperson Hua Chunying said this while commenting on WHO's declaration of coronavirus outbreak as public health emergency.

She said Chinese government has taken the most comprehensive and rigorous prevention and control measures against Coronavirus with a strong sense of responsibility for people’s health.
