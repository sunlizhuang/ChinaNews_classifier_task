Check out the galloping horses and skilled riders on the snow-covered grassland in N China.

XILINHOT, Jan. 13, 2020 (Xinhua) -- A horse training activity was held at a horse farm in north China's Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on Sunday. Enjoy the magnificent winter view of horses galloping on the snow-capped prairie.
