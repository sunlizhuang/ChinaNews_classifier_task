The Office of China Programs and Department of Modern and Classical Languages will host the 2020 Chinese New Year Celebration from Jan. 29- 31. There will be a series of Chinese cultural activities from Chinese martial arts and dance performances, to dumpling cooking workshops. Attendees will get hands-on experience in most of the activities.

Jinzi Fan from the International Leadership and Training Center and Ningxia University faculty and students have put together a tai chi performance and workshop. They will also teach the audience fundamental movements of tai chi.
