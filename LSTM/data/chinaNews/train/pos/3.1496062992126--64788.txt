No case of coronavirus in Pakistan: Dr. Zafar

Special Assistant on National Health Services Dr. Zafar Mirza has said that Pakistan stands by China to combat the coronavirus.

He was talking to Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jing in Islamabad on Monday.

The Special Assistant said Pakistani students in China are our children and we shall take every possible step to protect them.

He expressed the confidence that the Chinese government would soon win the fight against the coronavirus outbreak.

Zafar Mirza confirmed that so far there have been no case of coronavirus in Pakistan.

Chinese Ambassador Yao Jing said all possible information regarding the virus will be provided to Pakistani government.

Special Assistant on Overseas Pakistanis Zulfiqar Bukhari was also present on the occasion.
