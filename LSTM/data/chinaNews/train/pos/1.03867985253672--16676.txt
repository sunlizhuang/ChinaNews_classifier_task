Alex Azar, HHS Secretary, spoke with Mary Walter on The Brian Kilmeade Show about some of the healthcare accomplishments from the first three years of the Trump administration, including empowering Americans to be in control of their healthcare choices, lowering healthcare costs and other key healthcare issues. Secretary Azar also addressed the coronavirus in China. Azar explained how the CDC has implemented increased screening of at risk passengers at major airports to reduce the spread of the virus.
