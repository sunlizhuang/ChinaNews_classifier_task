The Amir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani held a telephone conversation with Chinese President Xi Jinping on Tuesday to offer his condolences and sympathy on the victims of the coronavirus outbreak, wishing the infected people a speedy recovery. During the call, the Amir voiced confidence in China’s ability to control this epidemic, stressing Qatar’s support and willingness to help in this regard.

For his part, the Chinese president extended thanks to the Amir for the support, and solidarity of Qatar with China and its assistance in transporting the epidemic control materials via Qatar Airways.

The phone call also touched on the strategic relations between the two countries, where the two leaders expressed satisfaction with the level they have reached in recent years, and confirmed their determination to develop them in all fields, especially with regard to the Silk Road initiative. (QNA)
