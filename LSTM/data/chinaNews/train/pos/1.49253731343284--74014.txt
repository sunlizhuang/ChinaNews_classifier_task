KABUL – Deputy and Acting Foreign Minister Mohammad Haroon Chakhansuri met with the Ambassador of the People’s Republic of China to Kabul Mr. Wang Yu to discuss a number of issues, particularly the outbreak of Coronavirus in China.

Ambassador Yu briefed Mr. Chakhansuri on the recent developments in Chinese Government’s scientific researches to find a vaccine for Coronavirus, treatment of patients suffering from this lethal virus, and the cooperation of the WHO in this regard.

Minister Chakhansuri thanked the Chinese government for its cooperation and support to the Afghan students in Wuhan province as well as continued coordination of the Chinese Embassy in Kabul with the Ministry of Public Health. He also called for further cooperation on this important issue.
