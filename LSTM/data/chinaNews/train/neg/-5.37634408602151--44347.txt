China grants 11-day visa extension to Pakistanis stranded in Urumqi

Pakistani students and community members stranded in Urumqi due to non-availability of flights in view of the new coronavirus outbreak in China have been granted an 11-day visa extension by the Chinese authorities.

All of them have been provided hotel accommodation and food will be made available for them till the flights resume between Urumqi and Islamabad.

The embassy officials are also in contact with them in Urumqi and there is absolutely no problem and difficulty being faced by these stranded people.
