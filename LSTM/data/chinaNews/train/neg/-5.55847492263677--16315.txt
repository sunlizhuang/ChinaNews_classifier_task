Passengers flying in from Wuhan are being monitored by Taiwan authorities.

A Taiwan woman has been confirmed to be the first person in the island infected with the new coronavirus in Wuhan. She had visited the provincial Chinese city recently.

The woman who works in Wuhan reported to quarantine officials at Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport that she had a fever on her return yesterday, according to an epidemic response command center established to contain the spread of the new coronavirus, CNA reports.

The woman was placed in quarantine and later tested positive for the pneumonia-like virus, the center said.
