A group of German nationals, including a Bulgarian with dual citizenship, are already at the Wuhan airport in China, where they will be evacuated by plane to Germany.

Upon arrival in Frankfurt, the group, together with other people arriving from China, will be placed in isolation and monitored for two weeks.

The other two Bulgarians based in Wuhan will be evacuated by French aircraft tomorrow, February 1.

In Bulgaria they will be accommodated in the Department of Infectious Diseases of the Military Medical Academy in Sofia in rooms with special access until the quarantine period is over.
