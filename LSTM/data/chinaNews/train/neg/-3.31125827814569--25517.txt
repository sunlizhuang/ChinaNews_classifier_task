The Wuhan coronavirus is spreading around the world.As of Sunday, more than two thousand people globally have been infected.The vast majority of them are in China, where 56 people have died.But the virus has also reached Europe, with at least three patients confirmed in France.Canada also reported its first case in a resident in his 50s who had returned from Wuhan.Hong Kong, with five confirmed cases, declared a virus emergency on Saturday and restricted links to mainland China.The virus has also been confirmed in Taiwan, Thailand, Japan, Singapore and the U.S.There has not yet been a confirmed case, however, in North Korea.
