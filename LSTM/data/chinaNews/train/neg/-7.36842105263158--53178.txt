Chinese community 'ostracised' over coronavirus fears, business leader says

Updated

Alex Lim from the Chinese Chamber of Commerce joins Virginia Trioli to speak about how the coronavirus is impacting the Chinese community in Box Hill.

The Chinese community has been ostracised, says Mr Lim, but they need to "play their part as well, not to be overwhelmed by fear."
