GHAZIABAD: The health department on Thursday rushed a quick response team to a flat in Vasundhara after it received a message from the Airports Authority of India that a 27-year-old city resident had landed in Delhi from China. By the time they reached her house, she had left for Delhi.CMO NK Gupta said she was studying in China and returned home on Wednesday. “In view of Wuhan coronavirus scare, we have orders that anyone coming from China should be screened and kept in isolation,” he said.Meanwhile, all three patients quarantined in Delhi’s RML hospital have tested negative.
