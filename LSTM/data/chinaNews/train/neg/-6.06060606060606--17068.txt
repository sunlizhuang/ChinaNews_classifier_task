Ren Zhengfei is investing in new areas to fill gaps caused by a US trade ban

Huawei will survive its difficulties and go on to create a new “army” that will “dominate the world”, its boss has said.

Ren Zhengfei, 74, a former officer in the People’s Liberation Army, told workers in a memo that they must enter “battle mode” to ride out the crisis.

The Chinese telecoms giant is under scrutiny across the West amid fears that its network equipment could be used by Beijing for espionage and sabotage. The British government is considering banning its equipment in the 5G network, strongly encouraged by the US.

Huawei said yesterday that US trade sanctions would cause revenue at its consumer business, which makes smartphones, to fall by at least $10 billion this year.
