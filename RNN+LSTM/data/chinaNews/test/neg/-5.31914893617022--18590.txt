> A Utah lawmaker is looking to increase the penalty for drivers who pass school buses while the red lights are flashing. Under the new bill, 1st time violators would have to pay $250 instead of $100. The fine for a 2nd offence would be $500 instead of the current $200. 3rd time offenders would be fined $1,000 fine instead of $500.
