KOCHI, India -- Undercurrent News is reporting live from the 22nd India International Seafood Show (IISS), taking place in Kochi, India from Feb. 7-9.

The biennial event has returned to the port city of Kochi for the first time in 12 years. The location, which has been visited for hundreds of years by Arab, Chinese and European merchants, is noted for its colonial bungalows, diverse houses of worship and iconic cantilevered fishing nets.

IISS, which encourages attendance from seafood processors, buyers, importers and others, will include technical sessions and also more than 250 exhibit stalls spread over a 7,000 square meter hall at the Grand Hyatt Bolgatty hotel.
