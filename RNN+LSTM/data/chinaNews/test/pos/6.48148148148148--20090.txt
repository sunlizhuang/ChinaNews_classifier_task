Celebrations for the Chinese New Year will kick off a little early with celebrations Thursday.

The festival centers on the start of the new year on the traditional Chinese calendar, which is this Saturday.

Thursday, the 'Them Society' in Gainesville will celebrate with a potluck, as well as a white elephant gift exchange for anyone interested in participating.

Information on the event can be found at https://www.facebook.com/events/1233452566849079/

The Newberry branch of the Alachua County Library will also celebrate Thursday by using their telescope to learn more about the moon.

Information on this event can be found at https://www.facebook.com/events/476189216659638/
