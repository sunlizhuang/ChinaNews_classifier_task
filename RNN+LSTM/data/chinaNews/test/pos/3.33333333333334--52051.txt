Riyadh: Saudi Arabia expressed support to China, backing its efforts to contain the novel coronavirus.

Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud voiced his country’s stance in a phone call with his Chinese counterpart Wang Yi.

The Chinese minister expressed deepest thanks to Saudi Arabia as China is going through difficult times in its struggle to contain the viral disease.

He hailed strategic partnership between the two countries, expressing his confidence that Saudi Arabia would stand by China.

In a statement posted on the ministry’s portal, Wang Yi said that his country is able to protect the safety of its people as well as Saudi nationals and people of other countries, Sabq reported.
