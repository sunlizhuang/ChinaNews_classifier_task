Paul Herbert flew to China in 2017, seeking a partner to help his precision engineering company expand. The chairman of Smiths Harlow met 10 Chinese manufacturers that promised to help him boost output. Future Aerospace, based in the southwestern province of Sichuan, seemed the best fit.

After being introduced through a government working group, Herbert met Han Hua, Future’s chairman, who took him to one of his factories near Beijing. “When you walked through the door, it looked just like Smiths,” Herbert recalled. “He came across as a very genuine guy.”
