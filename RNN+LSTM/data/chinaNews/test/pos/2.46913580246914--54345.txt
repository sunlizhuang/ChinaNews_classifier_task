BEIJING, Feb. 5 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Air Force's August 1st aerobatics team will attend the Singapore Airshow, scheduled for Feb. 11 through Feb. 16.

The August 1st team arrived in Singapore Wednesday and will give a performance at the air show, which will be the team's first appearance in the country.

Established in 1962, the team has staged more than 600 aerobatic performances for over 700 delegations from 168 countries and regions.
