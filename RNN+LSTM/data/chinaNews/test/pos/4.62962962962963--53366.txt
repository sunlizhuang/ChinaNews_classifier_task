Chinese media outlets have praised the UAE's support for the country in its fight against the new coronavirus.

They have highlighted the UAE’s gesture of illuminating important landmarks in the country as well as the messages in Mandarin.

The reports also hailed the messages of support from His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.

The UAE government has expressed its full confidence in China’s ability to manage the situation.

It’s also working to ensure that China has enough medical supplies to combat the outbreak.
