Jiangxi Provincial Port Group has been officially established to integrate local port resources in the region.

The newly established port group will be engaged in port and shipping infrastructure investment and construction, port operation and management, cargo transportation and logistics services.

The integration of local port resources is a strategic plan for Jiangxi province to improve the opening up and strengthen its ports competitiveness, said the group.
