North Country lawmakers gave Cuomo’s spending plan mixed reviews. They praised his goal of redistributing school aid, which would help many underfunded rural school districts in the region.

But they were skeptical of his plan to create a task force to identify $2.5 billion in Medicaid savings.

David Sommerstein spoke with Joe Griffo, a Republican State Senator who represents parts of St. Lawrence County and the Tug Hill Plateau. He’s also a leader in the GOP caucus in Albany.
