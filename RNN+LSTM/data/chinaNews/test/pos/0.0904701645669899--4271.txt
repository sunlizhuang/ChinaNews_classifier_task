Defense Minister Prabowo Subianto’s careful response to the tension between Indonesia and China over Chinese fishing vessels in Indonesia’s exclusive economic zone (EEZ) in the North Natuna Sea has drawn sharp criticism from some of the Gerindra Party chairman’s former allies. The Prosperous Justice Party (PKS), formerly one of Gerindra’s closest partners, issued a statement criticizing Prabowo’s perceived lack of firmness. Prabowo had said that the government would handle the situation in a “cool” manner. “We are cool. We are relaxed. We can resolve this amicably. After all, China is a friendly nation,” he said last week.
