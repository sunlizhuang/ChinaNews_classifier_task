In honor of the 70th Anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, People's Daily Online Australia produced the special edition of the talk show program Aussie Talks. In the past 70 years, China has changed so much astonishing the world. Nowadays, more and more foreigners want to travel to China, including many young Australians. In this episode, three young Australians Max, Amy and Rebecca will talk about interesting historical stories to us when they traveled to Beijing, Foshan and Macau in China. Let's see how they understand Chinese history through their experiences.
