MAST Foundation presents UNIFORM: INTO THE WORK / OUT OF THE WORK, an exhibition of work from 44 photography greats plus Walead Beshty. The exhibition showcases over 600 pieces of clothing and uniforms, worn by workers in different historical, social, and professional contexts. The exhibition, curated by Urs Stahel, displays the uniform in all of its symbolic and functional facets. It investigates the duality of these garments, designed on one hand to convey a sense of belonging, whilst on the other hand serving as a symbol of classification, singling out an individual or group from the rest of the community....
