“I remember when I was a kid, I was a big reader and I remember the ‘Read Aloud Book of President Lincoln,’ and the ‘Read Aloud Book of President Washington,’ and they were these little biographies on their life stories ... and they were supposed to morally inspire you to be like that person," Kaine said. “There is not one teacher or minister or parent who is saying to their children ‘You should act like President Trump. You should talk to people like President Trump does.’ I think there is a huge problem when the most exemplified American who represents America to the world has no character traits that are to be examples for young people.”
