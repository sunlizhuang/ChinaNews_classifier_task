External Affairs minister Jaishankar meets SCO secretary general Vladimir Norov

@DrSJaishankar

External Affairs minister S Jaishankar met Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) secretary general Vladimir Norov in New Delhi on Monday. Mr Jaishankar appreciated the SCO's efforts to promote tourism among member states. He said, the eighth wonder of SCO, which includes the Statue of Unity will surely serve as an inspiration.



AIR correspondent reports that they reviewed progress as India prepares to take up the chair of the Council of SCO Heads of Government. The Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO), is a Eurasian political, economic, and security alliance. India and Pakistan officially joined SCO as full members at a summit in Astana in 2017.
