Pakistan’s support to China in difficult time highlights deep friendship: China

Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi says Pakistan’s help and support to China in difficult time regarding coronavirus outbreak has once again highlighted deep friendship between two countries.

According to China’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs, while talking to Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi, Wang Yi said at a time when the Chinese people are experiencing difficulties, Pakistan has spared no effort to give China a helping hand.

He said that Pakistan has nearly a thousand citizens in Wuhan, and the Pakistani government stated that it has no intention to rush them out, which fully reflected its trust in the Chinese government.

He said China will continue to safeguard the safety and health of Pakistani brothers and sisters and provide them with all necessary assistance.
