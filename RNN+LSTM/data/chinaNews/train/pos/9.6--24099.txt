Armenian Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan sent congratulatory messages to the President Xi Jinping and Prime Minister Li Keqiang of the People’s Republic of China (PRC) on the occasion of the Chinese New Year.

“I convey warm greetings and wishes to You and the friendly people of China on the occasion of the New Year and the Spring holiday. The Spring festival comes from centuries and at the same time is one of the most cherished family holidays in China today. Let it bring health, new achievements and happiness to all families. Armenia attaches importance to a steady development of the friendly relations with China, “ the message sent to President Xi Jinping read in part.
