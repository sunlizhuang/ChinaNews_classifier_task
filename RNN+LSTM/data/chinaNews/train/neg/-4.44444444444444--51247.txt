Chinese from Wuhan are denied entry to Malaysia.

A total of 147 Chinese were barred from entering Malaysia between January 27 and Monday, the country's home minister said today. They were all from Wuhan.

"[65 of them] were stopped at Port Klang. They comprise sailors and crew members of ships docking at ports there. "[Others were stopped at] the Sultan Abu Bakar Complex in Gelang Patah [30 people] and klia2 [14]," Muhyiddin Yassin said, the New Straits Times reported.

Last week, Malaysia imposed a temporary ban on Chinese arriving from Hubei in an effort to contain the spread of the coronavirus.
