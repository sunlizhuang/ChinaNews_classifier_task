Kochi: One person in Kochi has been admitted to the Kalamassery medical college under the suspicion that he is affected with the coronavirus spread in China.

The youth, who recently returned from China, had first sought treatment at a private hospital in Kochi with high fever and cough. Doctors shifted him to the medical college immediately after suspecting it to be a case of coronavirus.

He has been kept under observation at the isolation ward set up at the hospital. His samples will be collected and sent to the National Institute of Virology in Pune for further examination. More details are awaited.

Meanwhile, a student in Ettumannoor who recently returned from China is also being monitored.

However, District Medical Officer has informed that she is completely fine now and all precautionary measures have been taken.
