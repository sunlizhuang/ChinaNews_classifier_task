Edmonton Chinatown Multicultural Centre will host the event to celebrate the Lunar New Yea , which is the most celebrated festival in Chinese community, every year with the public. It includes a 2 days event with different performances all day round and we also have different booths selling different kinds of items like fashions, Chinese craft, food, tea, Chinese herbs, honey etc. Performances will include both traditional cultural performance, ku fung, live band, pop songs etc. The highlight will be a multicultural show held on Saturday night which include different ethnic groups to perform and showcase their traditional culture. The purpose is to bring different cultures into one place and celebrate together.
