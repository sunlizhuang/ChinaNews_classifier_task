Definition:

Clay is a type of material with many beneficial properties like viscosity, plasticity, fired strength and many more. Because of these properties, clay is used for making pottery, both utilitarian and decorative, and construction products, such as bricks, wall and floor tiles. Varieties of clay, when used with different minerals and firing conditions, are used to produce earthenware, stoneware, and porcelain.
