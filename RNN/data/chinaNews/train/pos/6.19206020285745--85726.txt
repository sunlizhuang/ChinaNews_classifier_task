China welcomes peace deal between US, Afghan Taliban

China says it believes that peace treaty between US-Taliban would pave way for a smooth and orderly withdrawal of foreign troops stationed in Afghanistan.

In a press conference in Beijing, Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Zhao Lijian said they welcome the news that the United States and the Afghan Taliban are going to sign a deal.

He hoped that the agreement will be smoothly implemented to prepare ground for a political settlement of the Afghanistan issue.

He reiterated China's firm support for an Afghan-led and Afghan-owned broad and inclusive process for peace and reconciliation in Afghanistan.
