Chinese smartphone brand Realme has expanded its product portfolio in India with the launch of Realme 5i. Powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 octa-core chipset, the smartphone offers 6.52-inch HD+ screen.Realme has announced only one variant of Realme 5i in India. The handset packs 4GB RAM and 64GB internal storage that come with a price tag of Rs 8,999. It will go on its first sale on January 15 via Flipkart and Realme.com.As an introductory offer, Reliance Jio users will get benefits worth Rs 7,750 on the purchase of Realme 5i. Additional benefits include offers from Mobikwik and Cashify.Realme 5i runs on ColorOS 6.1 out-of-the-box and is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 AIE octa-core processor clocked at 2.0GHz. The device is backed by 5000mAh battery capacity with reverse charging support. Users will get a 10W charger with the phone.The smartphone comes with LPDDr4X RAM. It boasts an AI quad camera setup at the back. This consists of 12MP primary lens, 8MP ultra-wide lens with 119 degree field of view, 2MP Portrait lens and 2MP macro lens. Super Nightscape 2.0, EIS support for video and 240FPS slow motion video are the camera features available. For selfies, the handset comes equipped with an 8MP front sensor.The handset comes with a 6.52-inch HD+ mini-drop style screen with 720x1600 pixel resolution. Realme 5i comes with a triple card slot with an expandable storage of up to 256GB. The smartphone features what the company calls ‘Sunrise design’ with a mirror polished rear side. Aqua Blue and Forest Green are the color options. The company claims that the phone comes with an anti-fingerprint layer to keep the phone’s back smudge free. It also features a splash-resistant body.
