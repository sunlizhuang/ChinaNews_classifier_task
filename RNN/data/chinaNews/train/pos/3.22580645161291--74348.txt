Hundreds gathered at Jerusalem’s Western Wall on Sunday to hold a mass prayer for those impacted by the coronavirus outbreak.

John Zo, a student from China who attended the service, expressed his appreciation to the rabbis and the Jewish People for their warmth and concern, calling the prayer event a “very friendly action.”
