BEIJING, Feb 11 (Reuters) - China's Hubei province, the epicentre of the coronavirus outbreak, dismissed the provincial health commission's party boss Zhang Jin and director Liu Yingzi, state media CCTV reported on Tuesday.

Wang Hesheng, a member of the provincial committee of the Communist Party in Hubei province who has served in the public health system for years, will take over their roles, CCTV reported. (Reporting by David Stanway and Yilei Sun; Editing by Muralikumar Anantharaman)
