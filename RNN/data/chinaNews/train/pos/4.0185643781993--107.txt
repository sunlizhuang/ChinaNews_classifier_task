BEIJING, Jan. 6 (Xinhua) -- A compilation of Chinese President Xi Jinping's speeches marking the 70th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China (PRC) has been published by the Foreign Languages Press.

In 2019, Xi, also general secretary of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and chairman of the Central Military Commission, attended a host of celebrations for the 70th anniversary of the founding of the PRC and delivered important speeches.
