Vadodara: Health department officials of the Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) are keeping a close tab on the health of two individuals from the city who returned recently from China. The condition of the two individuals is said to be healthy so far.VMC officials said that the two men who were around 40-year-old had returned from China after a business trip. Their details were shared with the VMC by state health officials. After they arrived last week, the health officials are keeping in touch with them and inquiring about any ailments.“So far, they have not shown any flu-like symptoms or respiratory ailments. We have to monitor them for 28 days,” said an official adding that as per protocols their details could not be disclosed.
