A woman who was brought to Nebraska Medicine in Omaha from Camp Ashland does not have coronavirus.

She is one of 57 people being quarantined for the virus at the Nebraska National Guard facility in Ashland.

The woman was taken to Omaha on Friday after developing extremely mild respiratory symptoms and officials announced on Saturday that she was taken back to the quarantine site after testing negative.

The people under quarantine in Nebraska arrived in Omaha from the Wuhan area of China on February 7th. They are ten days into their two week quarantine.
