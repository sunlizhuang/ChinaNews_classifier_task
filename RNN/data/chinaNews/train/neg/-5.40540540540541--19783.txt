The Shandong provincial government held a press conference announcing that the province’s 2018 GDP numbers had to be revised down. Shandong’s GDP in 2018 is now estimated at roughly RMB 6,664.9 billion – a reduction of RMB 982.1 billion. Shandong Stats Bureau Deputy Director Lu Wanming explained that the department was looking to correct errors in its data collection process stemming from problems with source data and “the complexity of survey objects.”
