THE LATEST ON THE CORONAVIRUS OUTBREAK

* China reports 108 more deaths on Tuesday, taking its toll to 1016

* There were 2478 new confirmed cases on the mainland on Monday, down from 3062 the previous day, bringing the total to 42,638

* Hubei province, the epicentre of the outbreak, reported 2097 new cases and 103 new deaths

* There are 319 cases in 24 other countries and territories

* Hubei province has dismissed the Communist Party boss and a senior health official amid widespread criticism of the handling of the epidemic

* The 13th case in the United States was detected in California in a person under federal quarantine after returning from Wuhan, China

* Taiwan raised its travel warning on Tuesday, advising people not to visit Hong Kong or Macau and to take precautions if going to Singapore or Thailand

* Four people evacuated from a residential building in Hong Kong where a man and a woman confirmed with coronavirus lived have showed symptoms

* Vietnam has confirmed another case, bringing its total to 15.

* Thailand has recorded one new case, bringing its total to 33, and barred passengers from Holland America's cruise ship MS Westerdam from disembarking

* Asian share markets followed Wall Street higher even as doubts grew about how quickly China's factories could get back to work

* The WHO said 168 labs globally have the right technology to diagnose the virus

* The World Bank is offering technical assistance to China but no new loans

* It's feared the virus outbreak could trim China's full-year economic growth rate by as much as 1 percentage point

* Britain has declared the coronavirus a serious and imminent public health threat.
