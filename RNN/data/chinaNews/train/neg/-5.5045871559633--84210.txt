The employee of an online book retailer has led to the quarantine of 82 people after a woman became infected with the new coronavirus, health authorities said yesterday, Xinhua reports.

Doctors confirmed on February 19 that an employee of Dangdang.com, surnamed Li, was infected, said Pang Xinghuo, deputy director of the Beijing Center for Diseases Prevention and Control.

Li went back to the northeastern province of Heilongjiang on January 21 and returned to Beijing on February 5. She worked at the company from February 10 to 13 by public transportation.

Li had close contact with 82 people by Saturday. These people are under observation.
