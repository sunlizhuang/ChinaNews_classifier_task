ITHACA, N.Y. (WHCU) – Test results come back negative for a pair of individuals in Broome County who were tested for Coronavirus.

Spectrum News says the two returned from China and were experiencing respiratory distress. Health Officials in Broome County say the risk of coronavirus is low, however it’s not abnormal to have patients who qualify for testing.

New York State and local officials are encouraging education to calm any fears. Symptoms are similar to the flu, such as cough, sore throat and fever.
