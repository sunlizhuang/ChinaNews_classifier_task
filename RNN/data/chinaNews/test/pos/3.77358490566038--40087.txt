Chinese Ambassador to Lebanon Wang Kejian announced that China and Lebanon are interested in deepening cooperation under the Belt & Road Initiative (BRI). Wang made the remarks during his visit to the newly appointed Prime Minister Hassan Diab, who spoke positively of the bilateral ties between the two nations. Diab further thanked China for its support in the political, economic, military and humanitarian fields, and expressed hope for further progress in bilateral cooperation in various fields. Wang also visited Foreign Affairs Minister Nassif Hitti, with whom he discussed the possibility of how to promote and continue to develop bilateral ties.
