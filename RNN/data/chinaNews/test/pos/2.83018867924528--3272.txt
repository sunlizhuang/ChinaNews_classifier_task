Media reporters work at Luohuaying Village in Zhangbei County of Zhangjiakou City, north China's Hebei Province, on Jan. 7, 2020. Media workers started a nationwide reporting campaign on Tuesday that aims to produce in-depth coverage of China's poverty reduction practice and achievement. China is poised to tackle the hardest phase in securing a full victory in the anti-poverty battle in 2020 as it enters the homestretch in achieving the first centenary goal of building a moderately prosperous society in all respects.
