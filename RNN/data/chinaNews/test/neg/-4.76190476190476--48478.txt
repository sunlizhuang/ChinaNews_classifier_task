China has completed work on an emergency 1,000-bed hospital set specifically to battle the coronavirus in Wuhan. Work began on Huoshenshan Hospital on 23 January and was completed on Sunday morning. The hospital has been passed into the control of medical staff and will admit patients starting on Monday. It has an area of 25,000 sq. m. and 1,000 beds. Chinese authorities are building a second emergency hospital 25 miles away from the first, which is set to open on Wednesday.
