The United States and China are preparing to sign a "phase one" agreement in their trade war later this Wednesday. It will see Beijing commit to buying $200 billion more of American exports, while Washington will cut tariffs on more than $100 billion of Chinese goods. We hear from our Beijing correspondent Charles Pellegrin about some of the issues left unresolved by this deal.
