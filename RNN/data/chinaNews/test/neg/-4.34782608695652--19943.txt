A decontamination exercise was carried out at Dublin's Mater Hospital yesterday, after a patient was admitted with a suspected case of the coronavirus.

The woman had returned from China, and had a fever.

She was assessed and found not to have the virus.

According to the Irish Independent, an ambulance was decontaminated by staff as a precaution.

There are now more than 500 confirmed cases of coronavirus, and 17 people have died.
